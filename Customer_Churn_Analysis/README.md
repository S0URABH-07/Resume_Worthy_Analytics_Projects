# Enterprise Customer Churn Analytics & Subscription Optimization Engine

An advanced, production-grade churn optimization pipeline tracking subscription account attrition vectors, operational friction points, contractual structures, data cleaning mechanics, and retention indicators using Seaborn.

## Core Engineered Architectural Modules
* **Defensive Pipeline Engineering:** Implements secure split-step validation transformations on raw features (`Support Calls`, `Total Spend`) to isolate calculations from standard text dtype errors.
* **Complex Multi-Variable Feature Engineering:** Builds predictive indices including the `Spend_Velocity` metric (monthly monetary momentum tracking) and the `Customer_Friction_Score` vector.
* **Corporate Reporting Delivery Suite:** Automatically isolates and outputs 4 standalone high-resolution charts, 1 combined courtroom-ready executive dashboard grid layout, and processed logs.

## Core Strategic Discoveries & Storytelling Insights
1. **The Support Friction Threshold Vector:** Subscriber attrition patterns are closely bound to mechanical operational variables. The density distribution curve confirms that subscribers crossing 4 customer support contacts reach an exponential churn velocity threshold.
2. **Contract Type Attrition Mitigation Strategy:** Rolling monthly contract structures generate severe systemic volume leaks compared to fixed long-term agreements. Growth teams should configure automated discounting notifications targeting mid-tier accounts ($75+) in their first quarter to smooth out early lifecycle attrition.
